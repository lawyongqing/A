This one of the poster I do for the assignment of my subject Design For Print. This poster is made for the Koi The contest, the topic is pollution of ocean and how plastic is bad for life inside the sea. In the poster it shows that the Koi bear was making their plastic cup using PET recyclable plastic resin.

I start by doing research and chosse my color scheme, then I decide to do it in 3D illustration artstyle so I make a grid map and do rough sketch on it

Then I choose where the light source come from so I can use the gradient tool to add shadow to create value in the poster. And also add more detail to the bear.At last I add the logo and my slogan on the wall
