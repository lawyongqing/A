For this one I find my friend to help me to splash the water with a stick, then I use f/7.1, 1/1600 sec, ISO 500. The shutter speed is fast so it can capture the water in 1/1600 sec it looked like the water is freezing in the mid air.

This one is fun to take, it take at least two person to do it I use f/22, 13 sec, ISO 100 and use a tripod so my camera can stay still. The shutter speed is 13 sec which mean the camera will absorb the light source for 13 seconds. So I let my friend to stay still when I click the shot button I have 13 seconds to draw anything in my photo using any light source I use my phone flashlight to draw a pattern following his shadow in this case.
